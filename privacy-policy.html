Privacy Policy

Your privacy and security are important to us. Please read this policy carefully to understand how Paydrak operates.

Last Updated: November 17, 2025

1. Service Overview

Paydrak is a peer-to-peer (P2P) facilitation platform that connects users with third-party P2P traders on external cryptocurrency exchanges including Binance, Bybit, and other similar platforms. We are not a licensed financial institution, payment gateway, or money service business.

Our role is limited to providing a user interface and facilitating connections between users and independent P2P traders. All transactions occur directly between you and third-party traders on external platforms.

Important Disclaimers

Please read carefully:

• Paydrak does NOT hold, store, or have custody of your funds at any time

• We are NOT a payment processor, financial institution, or money transmitter

• We do NOT provide any financial, investment, or trading advice

• We are UNLICENSED and operate solely as a facilitation tool

• All transactions are conducted at YOUR OWN RISK

2. How Paydrak Operates

Paydrak acts as a facilitation tool only. When you use our platform: We connect you with independent P2P traders on platforms like Binance and Bybit. We provide rate information and facilitate communication. All monetary transactions occur directly between you and the third-party trader. We do not process, hold, or transfer any funds on your behalf. We do not have access to your exchange accounts or wallets. We are not a party to your transactions with P2P traders.

3. No Custody or Control of Your Funds

Paydrak NEVER holds your money. We are not a custodial service. When you initiate a transaction: Your funds go directly to the third-party P2P trader's account. We do not act as an intermediary for fund transfers. We have no ability to reverse, cancel, or modify completed transactions. We cannot recover funds sent to incorrect accounts or lost in transactions. All transactions are final and irreversible once executed.

Paydrak bears NO responsibility or liability for any losses, damages, or adverse outcomes resulting from your use of our platform or your transactions with third-party traders.

We do NOT collect or store sensitive financial information such as bank account numbers, card details, or cryptocurrency wallet private keys.
