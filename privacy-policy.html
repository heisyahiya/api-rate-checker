2. How Horizon Operates

Horizon acts as a facilitation tool only. When you use our platform:

  • We connect you with independent P2P traders on platforms like Binance and Bybit
  • We provide rate information and facilitate communication
  • All monetary transactions occur directly between you and the third-party trader
  • We do not process, hold, or transfer any funds on your behalf
  • We do not have access to your exchange accounts or wallets
  • We are not a party to your transactions with P2P traders

3. No Custody or Control of Your Funds

Horizon NEVER holds your money. We are not a custodial service. When you initiate a transaction:

  • Your funds go directly to the third-party P2P trader's account
  • We do not act as an intermediary for fund transfers
  • We have no ability to reverse, cancel, or modify completed transactions
  • We cannot recover funds sent to incorrect accounts or lost in transactions
  • All transactions are final and irreversible once executed
